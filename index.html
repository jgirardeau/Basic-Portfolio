About Me

Jim Girardeau is the CTO/Founder of Uptime Solutions, Inc. Uptime Solutions is a privately funded company developing products for wireless machinery management.

Previously Jim was a the VP of Engineering of ViXS systems, and managed multiple worldwide design centers in th areas of digital processing, video processing, and analog systems.

Jim was a Principal Engineer at Intel, a sr. digital designer at Level One Technologies and Motorola, Inc.
